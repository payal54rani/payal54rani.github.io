About Me

Hello! I am Payal Rani, a passionate Speech-Language Pathologist based in Bengaluru. With a Bachelor of Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology (BASLP) degree from Dr. S.R. Chandrashekhar Institute of Speech and Hearing and hands-on experience at renowned foundations like Akshadhaa and Spastic Society of Karnataka, I am dedicated to helping individuals overcome communication challenges.

My approach combines clinical expertise with compassionate care. I specialize in creating Individualized Education Plans (IEPs) for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder, speech delays, and behavioral issues. I believe in empowering parents through counseling and home programs to ensure holistic development for every child.

Professional Experience

Speech-Language Pathologist

Akshadhaa Foundation April 2023 – Present

Leading speech therapy initiatives for children with diverse needs.

  • Therapy Cycle & IEPs: Manage the academic year (May-April) with a structured quarterly cycle:
    • Month 1: Conduct IEP meetings and set 3-4 targeted goals based on current capabilities.
    • Month 2 (Demo Sessions): Parents observe sessions to understand techniques. I demonstrate interventions and guide parents to replicate them at home (e.g., using a structured "corner table" setup instead of allowing roaming).
    • Month 3: Finalize progress reports and update goals for the next quarter.
  • Clinical Assessment: Conduct detailed initial assessments checking oral motor skills (lateralization, protrusion, elevation), vegetative skills (chewing, biting, blowing), and sensory issues (texture sensitivity).
  • Intervention Tools: Utilize Oral Motor Tools in every session. Use Oral Swabs to reduce sensory sensitivity and Jaw Grading/Biting Blocks for biting and chewing rehabilitation.
  • Parent Counseling: Provide home programs and behavioral strategies, such as advising parents on creating strict, structured environments to improve child compliance and understanding.
  • Collaboration: Participate in monthly team meetings to discuss emotional or parental issues and coordinate holistic care.
